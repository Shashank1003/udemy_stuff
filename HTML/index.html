Roronoa Zoro

Vice captain of Straw Hats Pirates

Roronoa Zoro was a bounty hunter from the East Blue. His childhood competitor died in an accident which motivated Zoro to become world's strongest swordsman. He met Monkey. D. Luffy in Shells Town and defeated Morgan inorder to establish peace. Then he joind Luffy and became the first mate of the captain.
